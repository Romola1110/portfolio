I am Yang Xueqi, twenty-two, currently studying English Literature at CUHK.
I love the small light hidden in words, and the quiet brightness of daily life:
I read poems, write stories, make things by hand, and listen for the gentle voices in old pages;
I wander into museums to hear what objects whisper across time.
Quiet and warm among friends, attentive and stubborn in creation.
May this be a small garden where we share a few good moments.
